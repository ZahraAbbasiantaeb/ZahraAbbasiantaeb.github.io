About Me

I'm Zahra, a graduate of Artificial Intelligence from the Computer Engineering Department of the Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnics), where I also obtained my B.Sc. degree in Software Engineering. Currently, I am working as a Research Assistant at NLP Lab, under supervision of Dr. Saeedeh Momtazi. Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Information Retrieval (IR) are my main research interests. During my work as a Research Assistant, I had the chance to explore multiple directions in NLP and IR, namely, Recommender Systems, Question Answering Systems, and Expert Finding. These days I'm working on a book about Question answering Systems and a paper on Answer Sentence Selection.
