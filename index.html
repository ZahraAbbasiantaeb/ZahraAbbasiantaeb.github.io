About Me!

I am Zahra, a researcher at LIACS (Leiden Institute of Advanced Computer Science). As a researcher at LIACS, I am working on a Patent Mining project funded by EPO. I have completed my MSC with focus on Question Answering systems at the Computer Engineering Department of the Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnics) in the field of Artificial Intelligence. I have also obtained my B.Sc. degree in Software Engineering from AUT.
